Mission

At Fruition Cosmetics, we blend the vibrant essence of fruits with timeless elegance to create high-quality, playful, and sophisticated makeup. Our products are designed for young adults who love beauty that’s both fun and refined, empowering them to express themselves with confidence and creativity.

Logos

Logos

The Fruition Cosmetics logo was thoughtfully crafted to reflect the brand’s essence: a blend of elegance and playful energy. The primary typeface, Ivymode, brings a sense of sophistication and refinement, appealing to young adults. Playful, natural touches, like the leaf-shaped tittle accents over the "i"s, introduce a fresh and organic feel. The secondary small logo takes inspiration from fruit, reinforcing the brand’s core theme of beauty rooted in nature.
